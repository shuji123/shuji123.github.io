Biography

Hi, I am Shūji Ōtomo. My research interests are unsteady aerodynamics, flow control, and experimental fluid mechanics.

I am currently a research assistant at VOILAb at School of Engineering at the University of Edinburgh

After completing my BSc in Mechanial Engineering at Hokkaidō University, I left for Scotland to undertake a PhD in unsteady aerodynamics at the University of Edinburgh.
Since October 2020, I was appointed as a research assistantto to develop a low-order model for a novel tidal energy harvesting device for a company Tidal Flyer.
