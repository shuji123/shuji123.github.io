Eiffel type Wind Tunnel

We hand-made an Eiffel-type wind tunnel named "SEKIREI (鶺鴒) Wind tunnel". It has a 0.4 m x 0.4 m x 1.5 m test section and maximum velocity of approximately 20 m/s. Typical turbulence intensity is 1%. It is equipped with louvres that can change the freestream velocity and incoming wind angle.
